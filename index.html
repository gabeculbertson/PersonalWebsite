Biography

Gabriel is currently a PhD student in Cornell's Information Science department. Gabriel completed his BS in Mechanical Engineering from Purdue University with minors in Computer Science, Asian Studies and Global Engineering. His research interests include cross-cultural communication and education technology. He is particularly interested in exploring new ways to allow people to work across cultural boundaries. He is currently working on projects examining the role of affect in determining the effectiveness of multi-cultural teams. His other research is looking at new approaches to language learning technology.
